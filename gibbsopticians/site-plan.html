About Us

About us
Here at Gibbs Opticians, we take pride in our profession of optometry and in going the extra mile to ensure that you enjoy a clear and comfortable vision. Our experienced and highly qualified optometrists first deeply understand your vision and eye health needs and then use the latest innovative and ethical technologies to conduct thorough eye examinations to come up with an optimal spectacle prescription that will address your visual needs.
Our Vision:
To become a global trusted brand in vision care.
Our Mission:
Providing high-quality, affordable vision care using the latest, innovative, and ethical technologies, by highly qualified, experienced, and friendly professionals.
Our History:
Gibbs Opticians was established in 2019 to meet the needs to persons seeking quality eye care that is provided with a humane touch. Since then, we have been successfully provided care to over two thousand happy clients and the numbers are still growing.
Dr. Gika Chizim
Dr. Gika Chizim the founder, principal optometrist, and managing director of Gibbs Opticians holds a Doctor of Optometry degree from the University of Benin, Benin City, Nigeria, and is an alumnus of the Vision Care Institute and has over twenty years of optometry practice in both public and private sector locally and internationally. Prior to starting his private practice, Dr. Chizim worked extensively in the Middle East in such countries as the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates where he worked with leading optical chains like Yateem Group and Vision Express and rose to the position of senior optometrist and head of professional affairs. He has a keen interest in myopia control, specialty contact lens practice and diseases of the anterior segment. He is passionate about helping patients obtain optimal vision for maximum productivity at work and leisure.

Our Services

Our Services.
Our focus is the provision of primary eye care services which apart from ensuring a clear and comfortable vision, screens to eyes for such eye diseases as glaucoma, macular degeneration, retinitis pigmentosa, and other serious conditions that could lead to blindness. Apart from these, we can also get a general overview of a patient’s general health conditions through a routine eye examination. For example, do you know that such diseases like diabetes, high blood pressure, and hyperthyroidism affect the eyes and during a routine eye examination, such diseases can be detected and appropriate referrals made to start treatment thereby preventing devastating effects?
The following are the services offered at Gibbs Opticians:
Primary Eye Care:
We conduct detailed general eye examination which includes refraction, and ophthalmoscopy to determine the patient’s eye health and refractive status. After the examination, we take our time to explain our findings and provide the necessary counseling.
Contact Lens Practice:
At Gibbs Opticians we have a special interest in contact lens practice. From thorough eye examination to determining suitability for contact lens wear, getting the right contact lens prescription to meet your visual needs, and counseling and training on contact lens insertion, removal, and care, our staff members are with you all the way through your contact lens journey.
Optical Dispensing:
We dispense high-quality ophthalmic lenses. Be it prescription polarized lenses, anti-fog lenses, light adaptive lenses, and safety lenses for industrial uses, we offer the right lenses to meet your needs.
Designer Frames and Sunglasses:
We stock the latest and trending collection of sunglasses and frames
HMOs
We are optical service providers to highly reputable HMO companies like Mediplan, Reliance HMO, Hallmark HMO, Novo Healthcare Africa, Hygeia, SUNU, Avon with many more coming soon.
