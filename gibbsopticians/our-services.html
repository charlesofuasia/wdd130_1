Our Services

Our focus is the provision of primary eye care services which apart from ensuring a clear and comfortable vision, screens to eyes for such eye diseases as glaucoma, macular degeneration, retinitis pigmentosa, and other serious conditions that could lead to blindness. Apart from these, we can also get a general overview of a patient’s general health conditions through a routine eye examination. For example, do you know that such diseases like diabetes, high blood pressure, and hyperthyroidism affect the eyes and during a routine eye examination, such diseases can be detected and appropriate referrals made to start treatment thereby preventing devastating effects?
