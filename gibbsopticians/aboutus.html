About us

Gibbs Opticians was established in 2019 to meet the needs to persons seeking quality eye care that is provided with a humane touch. Since then, we have been successfully provided care to over two thousand happy clients and the numbers are still growing.

Here at Gibbs Opticians, we take pride in our profession of optometry and in going the extra mile to ensure that you enjoy a clear and comfortable vision. Our experienced and highly qualified optometrists first deeply understand your vision and eye health needs and then use the latest innovative and ethical technologies to conduct thorough eye examinations to come up with an optimal spectacle prescription that will address your visual needs.

Dr. Gika Chizim the founder, principal optometrist, and managing director of Gibbs Opticians holds a Doctor of Optometry degree from the University of Benin, Benin City, Nigeria, and is an alumnus of the Vision Care Institute and has over twenty years of optometry practice in both public and private sector locally and internationally. Prior to starting his private practice, Dr. Chizim worked extensively in the Middle East in such countries as the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates where he worked with leading optical chains like Yateem Group and Vision Express and rose to the position of senior optometrist and head of professional affairs. He has a keen interest in myopia control, specialty contact lens practice and diseases of the anterior segment. He is passionate about helping patients obtain optimal vision for maximum productivity at work and leisure.
